about

DERSBY MEDICAL CARE is a concierge medical service established under the DERSBY GROUP of companies on the 1st of January, 2020, to provide standard world-class health care to our cherished clients, right at their doorstep. We, at DERBSY MEDICAL CARE, believe in holistic medical care and ease of access to high quality medical care regardless of your geographic location. We have a dedicated team of doctors, nurses, and other ancillary staff all over the country to provide that needed service. Our efforts are 100% dedicated to our brand and our clients, and our passion drives us to be the best at what we do.

Our Vision

To be the most respected and renowned provider of concierge medicine and medical equipment in Ghana and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with exceptional customer service and high quality medical equipment.

Core Values

We combine Integrity, Quality, Professionalism and Customer Service to give clients the best possible medical care.
