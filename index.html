about

DERSBY MEDICAL CARE is a concierge medical service established under the DERSBY GROUP of companies on the 1st of January, 2020, to provide standard world-class health care to our cherished clients, right at their doorstep. We, at DERBSY MEDICAL CARE, believe in holistic medical care and ease of access to high quality medical care regardless of your geographic location. Our efforts are 100% dedicated to our brand and our clients, and our passion drives us to be the best at what we do.
